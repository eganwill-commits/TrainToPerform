Our Approach

A word before you pick a program.

This is for athletes who would rather follow a real framework than chase a new workout every Monday.

Most programs treat all athletes the same and simply adjust the volume, weight or time. T2P treats the framework as universal and adjusts the dose.

A teenager building capacity trains differently than an adult balancing performance with life demands. Same six pillars. Same sequence. Different weighting based on where you are now — and where you need to go next.

What you won't find here
  • Hero workouts
  • Personality-driven programming
  • Volume mistaken for progress
  • Hidden tiers or locked features

Every athlete receives the methodology in full. The price reflects the delivery: in person in Jackson, or custom remote programming built specifically for you.

Teens see the fastest results — biology rewards the right work at the right age, and the framework is built to take advantage of that window. But the principle holds at every stage. Consistency beats intensity. Training evolves as you do. The athletes who keep getting better are the ones who keep showing up after the early gains stop feeling automatic — at 16 and at 56.

If you're looking for a quick transformation, this probably isn't for you — and saying that up front is part of the deal.

Fine Print

The fine print, in plain English.

Things worth knowing before you start.
  • Free trial?

    Yes — 7 days on Adult and Masters digital programs. Full access during the trial. Cancel anytime before day 7 with no charge.

  • How does annual billing work?

    Paid once, upfront. No monthly auto-bill, no surprise renewals. You'll receive a renewal note 30 days before your next year begins.

  • Can I cancel or pause?

    Monthly digital subscriptions cancel anytime, no penalty. In-person cohorts run in 12-week blocks; mid-block pauses are handled case-by-case. Custom programming requires a 3-month minimum, after which it goes month-to-month.

  • Why is in-person more than digital?

    Because your coach is in the room. Each in-person cohort is capped at six athletes — that's a deliberate design choice, and it's reflected in the price.

  • How do I apply for Custom Programming?

    Brief application, then a free 20-minute call to make sure it's the right fit. Custom isn't sold via a Buy button — it's a real coaching relationship.

  • Sibling, family, or team rates?

    Sibling discount of 15% is automatic on the second Teen athlete. For families training across multiple programs (Teen + parent on Adult, etc.), reach out — happy to work something out.
